AFTERCARE GUIDE

Tattoo Aftercare Instructions

1. First 24 Hours:

• Keep the wrap on for 24 hours.2. After 24 Hours:

• Wash your hands.

• Remove the bandage.• Gently clean the tattoo with cool water and a paper towel.

• Pat it dry, let it air for one minute, then apply a thin layer of aftercare (just enough to make it shiny).3. First 48 Hours:

• Repeat the cleaning and aftercare process twice a day.4. After 48 Hours:

• Apply aftercare as needed to keep the tattoo moisturized.• If it looks dry, whitish, scaly, or rough, you’re not applying enough.

• Some people need to apply 2-3 times a day, others 5-6 times or more.5. Things to Avoid for 2 Weeks:

• Saunas, hot tubs, and swimming pools.

• Submerging the tattoo underwater (showers are fine).

• Using polysporin or Vaseline (these can harm the healing process).Key Tip:

Never let your tattoo dry out. Keep it moisturized and monitor how it’s healing.
